AirBNB Listings Dashboard
Using Tableau

In this project, I created a dashboard in Tableau
to better understand the real estate market and help decide on an
ideal property to invest in for as a rental unit.

  • View Dashboard

The raw data was obtained through a public dataset for 2016 data available on Kaggle.

The raw data consisted of Movie data with fields like Location, Bedrooms, Monthly and Daily Rent, Return on Investment, etc.

I decided to analyze the real estate market for AirBNB and create a dashboard to help me visualize the amount of cost and and profit I would receive based on the location and a variety of factors. I wanted to see how house hacking or side hustles that involved real estate could be profitable in various zip codes and throughout the year. Short answer: Real Estate can a fun side hustle... if the property is paid off.
